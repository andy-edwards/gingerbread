Unwind in the heart of Northumberland

Set on the lovely Felmoor Holiday Park in the heart of the superb countryside of Northumberland is the Scandinavian style Gingerbread Cabin. This luxury log cabin occupies a wonderful position looking out over the peaceful lake.

Sleeps six people

One large double room and two twin rooms mean a total of six people can stay with us

Dine together in style

A dining table seating six means all the family can dine together in style.

Fully fitted kitchen

Fully fitted kitchen equipped with all the appliances needed for a successful self catering break or longer holiday.

Stylish and relaxing living area

The living area is stylishly furnished to a high standard and has two comfortable sofas and a flat screen television set.

Relax and unwind in the hot tub

The cabin benefits from it's own 6 person hot tub. Why not turn on the bubbles and relax with a glass of wine

Enjoy the view

Gingerbread cabin looks out directly onto a calming tranquil lake. Watch the swans from the comfort of your veranda or hot tub, just don't forget to save them some breadcrumbs....
